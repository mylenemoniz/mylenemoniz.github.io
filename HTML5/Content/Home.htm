A Guide to All Things Accessibility

With this website, I hope to showcase my technical writing skills to potential employers and collaborators, as well as my adaptability when producing different types of content. The intention of this website is to showcase my knowledge in all things accessibility and to portray my ongoing learning/improvement in technological spaces as I advance in my studies and career.

The Libby App

Libby is a free application that provides access to a plethora of books, audiobooks, and magazines all at your fingertips. Whether you’re on the go or at your reading nook, the Libby app is perfect for finding your favorite titles. With accessibility best practices such as options to alter:

  • Color

    • Since Libby’s interface adapts to the colors of book covers and the user’s active library, this setting reduces such changes.

  • Text variation

    • Libby gives the user the option to use a single font at a single weight, with no italics, and rarely displays text in all-caps.

  • Motion

    • Animation effects may be a setting users wish to reduce or minimize.

  • Vibrations

    • This settings prevents haptic feedback generated by Libby.

  • Text scaling

    • The user is able to alter the text size of their text. Note: There is an option to include accessibility sizes which gives the user more text scaling options.

  • Light/dark mode

    • Change the appearance of your screen.

  • Screen orientation

    • Change the orientation in which graphics are displayed on your screen.

  • Typeface, justification, line spacing, and weight

    • Customize the formatting of your text.

 

With many options to alter your book display to your preferred settings, Libby is perfect for users who are looking for ways to enhance their reading experience. Libby also satisfies WCAG EPUB accessibility requirements such as:

  • Page navigation

    • Users are able to easily find and navigate through different sections of a text.

  • Reading order

    • Users are able to distinguish the order a text must be read.

  • Skippability

    • Users are able to skip over content.

  • Escapability

    • Users are able to exit out of structured content such as side bars, highlights, notes, etc.

  • Discoverability

    • Users are able to find different features.

 

Connecting your library card to Libby

  1. Download or open the Libby application on your device.

     

     

  2. Select Add Library.

     

     

  3. Type in the name of the library you have a card under.

  4. Select your library

     

    .

     

  5. Enter your library card number to sign into Libby. You have been brought to your library page and are now ready to begin browsing for your favorite titles.

     

 

You can stream titles on Libby with Wi-Fi or mobile data, or download them for offline use and read anytime.

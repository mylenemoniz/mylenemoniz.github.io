I Hate Writing Poems

i'm sitting here trying to write down how i feel

i've never tried to before, this doesn't feel real

i'm trying to write this poem and rhyme

how to people do this, how do they have the time


three semesters of teaching and work

you accepted us and all of our quirks

one of the first teachers to really see what i could do and who i could be

i'm constantly left wondering, does she really mean me?

clearly i'm no dr seuss

writing poetry is irritating, i feel so obtuse


we exist in a structure where we must play out our roles

and because of you i've reached one of my goals

that was a really cheap rhyme, sorry i'm trying to finish my finals and i'm in the middle of my grind

but i hope you don't mind


maybe i could have done this a different way

but hopefully you get what i'm trying to say



I'm the worst at writing poems, but I wanted to somehow show my appreciation in a way that meant something. Hope you enjoyed the worst poem ever written by yours truly.
